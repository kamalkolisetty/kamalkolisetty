## 🌟 My Epic Projects

These are my pride and joy—check out the **Repositories** tab for more!

### 📍 **DestNotify - Your Smart Travel Buddy**  
Missed your bus stop? Not anymore! This **Progressive Web App (PWA)** uses **Google Maps API** for pinpoint location alerts and **Gemini API** to suggest the best local eats, movies, and tips. Perfect for travelers who want to stay ahead!  
- **Tech Stack**: JavaScript, PWA, Google Maps API, Gemini API, EmailJS  
- **[Code It](https://github.com/kamalkolisetty/DestNotify)** | **[Try It Live](https://destnotify-prod-v2.netlify.app/)**  

### ✍️ **WindowsToo - Math Meets Magic**  
Draw a math problem, and watch this app solve it like a wizard! Built with **React + Vite** and a **FastAPI + Gemini AI** backend, it turns your sketches into crisp solutions with **MathJax**. Math just got fun!  
- **Tech Stack**: React.js, Vite, TypeScript, Python, FastAPI, Google Gemini AI, MathJax  
- **[Dive In](https://github.com/kamalkolisetty/kamal-windowsToo)** | **[See the Demo](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1u9SXeHG-7whiAqtuMKyySofwMUxIEvGJ/view)**  

### 📚 **BookWorm's Paradise - A Book Lover’s Dream**  
A slick **MERN stack** app for browsing and managing books or courses. With secure **bcrypt.js** authentication and a robust **MongoDB** backend, it’s your go-to for a cozy digital bookshelf.  
- **Tech Stack**: React, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Mongoose  
- **[Check It Out](https://github.com/kamalkolisetty/BookStore)**
